About Us

Founded by Adhi and Lithu, fin. STUDIOS is a Colombo-based creative media production house with a passion for storytelling through visuals. We specialize in photography, videography, and post-production, focusing on capturing raw emotion, aesthetic details, and authentic moments that resonate.

At fin. STUDIOS, we believe visuals are more than just frames — they’re experiences, memories, and narratives frozen in time. Whether it's a cinematic highlight reel, a heartfelt event, or a scroll-stopping reel for social media, our work is driven by intention and crafted with precision.

We collaborate with brands, artists, and individuals to bring visions to life — blending creativity with technical finesse. From concept to final cut, we handle the full journey.
